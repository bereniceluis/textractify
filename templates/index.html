how it works

Optical Character Recognition is the process of detecting and reading text in images through computer vision.

Image import

get images directly from your computer.

textractify can receive input with image formats such as JPEG, JPG, and PNG.

Image pre-processing

using OpenCV open-source library.

Image pre-processing are done to improve the chances of successful recognition. The aim of image pre-processing is to enhance the original input image to suppress unwanted distortions.

Character Recognition

with the help of pyTesseract wrapper for Google 's Tesseract OCR

This is the process where text is being recognized in the image input.

Output extracted text

Release it to the world...

Once the image has been put through final testing, the extracted text is now ready.

frequently asked questions

What is Optical Character Recognition?

It is a technology that recognizes text within an image. It is commonly used to recognize text in scanned documents and images.

What are the use cases for OCR Technology?

Most popular brands use OCR to initiate innovative campaigns to increase the engagement of their customers. OCR can also be used in payment processes and for identification processes such as IDs and passports.

How accurate is textractify?

textractify is not 100% guaranteed to be accurate, but textractify is continuously being improved by the developers since textractify started as a school project.

What are the tools used to make textractify possible?

textractify uses Python as the programming language, OpenCV for the pre-processing of images, pyTesseract wrapper to detect text in images and integrated it to Flask for the development of the website framework.

Why do I get bad results?

As mentioned before, textractify does not guarantee 100% accuracy. You might get bad results because the software might not always perform well on most images due to noise, skewed images, low resolution, text in natural scenes, and with borders. The accuracy is directly dependent on the quality of the image input.

What are the file extensions supported?

textractify only accepts file extensions for images such as png, jpg, and jpeg.
